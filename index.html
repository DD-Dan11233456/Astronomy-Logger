How to Use This Logger

  1. Configure Session Preferences: Type your target tracking location. Select whether to use your system's Auto time or pick a specific geographical region under Manual Select, then lock properties.
  2. Enter Celestial Data: Type your object coordinates into the operational matrix.
  3. Review Your Entry: Click Log Object. The system automatically stamps the transaction using your selected zone.
  4. Re-edit Rows: Hit a row's red Edit button to recall values into inputs. Resubmitting will update that specific index and refresh the timestamp to the current selected time zone matrix automatically.
  5. Export Your Log: Click Finish & Download TXT File to capture final run bounds and download your data file.
