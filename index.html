How to Use This Logger

  1. Set Location First: Type your tracking area and hit Lock Location & Start Session. This sets your global location and captures your exact session Start Time.
  2. Enter Celestial Data: Type your target object information into the coordinates fields.
  3. Review Your Entry: Click Log Object. The system automatically captures the exact timestamp the object was **Located**. Check the parameters on screen.
  4. Save or Modify: Click Confirm & Add to populate your spreadsheet list, or click Edit / Cancel to safely roll back and re-type the metrics.
  5. Export Your Log: Once completed with all records, click the purple Finish & Download TXT File button. This stamps your final session **End Time**, computes runtime, and generates a formatted logging sheet file.
