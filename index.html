How to Use This Logger

  1. Set Location First: Type your tracking area and hit Lock Location & Start Session. This captures your exact session Start Time.
  2. Enter Celestial Data: Type your target object information into the coordinate fields.
  3. Review Your Entry: Click Log Object. The system automatically captures the timestamp. Check your metrics.
  4. Save or Modify: Click Confirm & Add to populate your spreadsheet list.
  5. Re-edit Any Row: Need to change a logged object? Click its red Edit button in the table. The info will return to the input boxes. When you re-submit, its timestamp **automatically updates to the current time**.
  6. Export Your Log: Click the purple Finish & Download TXT File button at the bottom of the table to generate your clean final tracker document.
